Goal

Given a number N sum up all even numbers from 2 to N.

Input

Line 1 The number N

Output

Line 1 An integer K, the sum of all even numbers in the range 2 to N (Inclusive).

Constraints
2 = N =10000
2 = K = 25005000

Example

Input

10

Output

30
